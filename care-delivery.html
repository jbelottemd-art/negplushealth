Our Vision

A New Standard for Ambulatory Care

NEG Plus PLLC's care delivery segment is being built from the ground up with a clear thesis: women deserve a healthcare environment that combines the rigor of academic medicine, the warmth of patient-centered practice, and the efficiency of modern ambulatory design.

We are developing a two-service-line outpatient practice — OBGYN & Women's Health under Dr. Jimmy Belotte, and Primary Care under Dr. Mitchell Alexis — designed to function as an integrated, coordinated care platform rather than a collection of siloed visits.

Every design decision — from scheduling and workflow to the physical environment and care protocols — is being made with the patient experience at the center and evidence-based outcomes as the north star.

Service Lines

Two Specialties. One Coordinated Platform.

Our care delivery model integrates OBGYN & Women's Health with Primary Care — allowing patients to receive comprehensive, longitudinal care from a coordinated team.

Service Line 1

OBGYN & Women's Health

Led by Dr. Jimmy Belotte, MD, PhD, MBA, FACOG

Comprehensive, evidence-based gynecologic and women's health care delivered with clinical excellence, compassion, and a commitment to the whole person — not just the presenting complaint.

Planned Services
  • Reproductive health — contraception counseling, LARC, family planning
  • Sexual health & education
  • Preventive care — cancer screening & well-woman visits
  • Cervical dysplasia & microinvasive cancer treatment (colposcopy, LEEP)
  • Chronic conditions — PCOS, endometriosis, hormonal disorders
  • Menopause management & hormone therapy
  • Pelvic pain, abnormal uterine bleeding evaluation
  • Mental health screening & abuse support referrals
  • Minimally invasive gynecologic procedures
JB
Dr. Jimmy Belotte, MD, PhD, MBA, FACOG

FACOG · OBGYN & Women's Health Lead

Service Line 2

Primary Care

Led by Dr. Mitchell Alexis, MD, MS

Comprehensive adult primary care with an emphasis on preventive medicine, chronic disease management, and longitudinal patient relationships — integrated with our women's health service line.

Planned Services
  • Annual wellness exams & preventive screenings
  • Chronic disease management (HTN, diabetes, hyperlipidemia)
  • Vaccines & immunizations
  • Acute illness & urgent primary care
  • Mental health screening, counseling & referral coordination
  • Contraceptives counseling integrated with OBGYN team
  • Pain management & chronic pain coordination
  • Care navigation & specialty referral pathways
  • Integrated women's wellness (co-managed with OBGYN)
MA
Dr. Mitchell Alexis, MD, MS

Primary Care Lead · Care Delivery Co-Lead

Clinical Philosophy

How We Practice

The principles that guide every clinical decision at NEG Plus PLLC — from how we design our workflows to how we greet patients at the door.

Evidence-Based Practice

Every clinical decision is grounded in the best available evidence, current guidelines, and the individual context of each patient.

Shared Decision-Making

Patients are partners in their care — not recipients of it. We invest time in education, options, and the conversation that builds trust.

Equity & Cultural Humility

We serve all patients with the same standard of excellence — actively working to dismantle healthcare disparities in women's health and primary care.

Coordinated Care

OBGYN and Primary Care work together — sharing information, co-managing complex patients, and reducing the burden of navigation on patients.

⏱

Time & Respect

We design our schedules to respect patients' time — minimizing wait times, maximizing visit value, and being present in every interaction.

Outcomes-Driven

We measure what matters. Quality metrics, patient experience, and clinical outcomes guide continuous practice improvement.

About NEG Plus PLLC

A PLLC Built for the Next Era of Healthcare

New England Gynecology Plus PLLC is a physician-founded professional organization with a single, unifying mission: to expand access to preventative care and treatment, improve patient outcomes, and reduce cancer disparities in women's health.

Our vision is to be a lifeline of hope — bringing healing, comfort, and transformation to the lives of women who need it most. We pursue that vision through a forward-thinking integrated model that unifies care delivery, education, research, advocacy, technology, and strategic investment.

Founded by Dr. Jimmy Belotte, NEG Plus PLLC operates across two segments: a nationally focused consulting practice active today, and a regionally anchored care delivery platform — led by Dr. Belotte and Dr. Mitchell Alexis — expected to launch in 2027.

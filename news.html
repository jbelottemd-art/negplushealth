New Release — Life Sciences & Strategic Leadership

The Alchemy of Agency

Navigating the Labyrinth of Drug Development from Molecule to Metaphysics
A Strategic Guide for Life Sciences Professionals — from the Lab to the Boardroom and Beyond

A landmark work by Dr. Jimmy Belotte, MD, PhD, MBA, FACOG — physician-scientist, clinical development leader, and Founder of New England Gynecology Plus PLLC. The Alchemy of Agency introduces the Strategic Altitude Stack: a conceptual architecture ascending from molecules and mechanisms through clinical trials, commercial realities, enterprise governance, geopolitical constraints, and the philosophical implications of programmable biology. At every tier, a single question persists — who controls the ability to intervene in living systems, and on whose terms?

"Drug development is fundamentally a negotiation of agency — the ability to intervene in living systems — mediated by regulation, economics, culture, and risk tolerance."

— The Alchemy of Agency, Introduction

What's New

May 2026 Launch

negplushealth.com Goes Live

NEG Plus PLLC's new website is now live at negplushealth.com — featuring expanded content on drug development advisory, medical device consulting, life sciences capabilities, and the 2027 care delivery platform. The site reflects our full scope of physician-led services across consulting and care delivery.

Spring 2026 Speaking

Dr. Belotte Available for Keynote & Grand Rounds Engagements

Dr. Jimmy Belotte, MD, PhD, MBA, FACOG is actively accepting speaking engagements for 2026–2027. Topics include women's health strategy, gynecologic oncology, the intersection of medicine and capital markets, and physician executive leadership. Contact us to book.

2026 Care Delivery

Care Delivery Platform in Active Development — 2027 Launch

Site selection, credentialing, and service line design for NEG Plus PLLC's OBGYN and primary care practice are underway. Led by Dr. Belotte and Dr. Mitchell Alexis, the practice will serve patients across New England with a whole-person, unhurried care model. Interest registration is open now.

2026 Life Sciences

Life Sciences Advisory Practice Expanded

NEG Plus PLLC has formalized its drug development and medical device advisory capabilities — offering end-to-end consulting from concept and pre-clinical strategy through FDA submission, launch, and post-market lifecycle management. Biotech and MedTech organizations can now engage Dr. Belotte's team across the full development continuum.

About

Dr. Jimmy Belotte is a board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist and the founder and managing principal of New England Gynecology Plus PLLC. He brings together deep clinical expertise in women's health, gynecologic oncology, and minimally invasive surgery with rigorous business training — including an MBA with concentrations in healthcare management, corporate finance, and strategic management.

His career sits at the intersection of medicine, capital markets, and organizational leadership — serving health systems, biotech companies, academic medical centers, private equity investors, and C-suite and governance teams. Dr. Belotte's dual fluency — the clinical judgment of a practicing physician and the analytical sophistication of a trained strategist — is the defining foundation of NEG Plus PLLC.

Consulting Practice Focus

Dr. Belotte's consulting practice encompasses the full arc of strategic and financial decision-making in healthcare. He advises organizations on clinical strategy, market positioning, regulatory navigation, and institutional transformation — with particular depth in women's health, oncology, and the rapid commercialization of novel medical technologies.

Our Principles

What Guides Us

Across both consulting and care delivery, NEG Plus PLLC is held together by a set of principles that are non-negotiable — in how we advise, how we practice, and how we build.

Evidence Over Opinion

Every recommendation — clinical or strategic — is grounded in data, peer-reviewed literature, and rigorous analysis. We do not guess.

Partnership Over Transaction

Consulting clients and patients alike deserve advisors and physicians who are invested in their outcomes — not just their immediate request.

Dual Fluency

Medicine and business are not opposites. The ability to move between clinical judgment and financial analysis — without sacrificing depth in either — is what makes our model work.

Bias Toward Action

Strategy without execution is just planning. We are built to move organizations, institutions, and eventually patients from insight to outcome.

Integrity in Complexity

High-stakes decisions rarely have clean answers. We commit to intellectual honesty, transparent communication, and the courage to deliver difficult truths.

Equity by Design

Women's health has historically been under-resourced. Our care delivery and consulting work is intentionally built to close gaps — in access, research, and outcomes.

Excellence Without Compromise

The standard we hold ourselves to in clinical care is the same standard we apply in every strategic engagement. Good enough is not our benchmark.

Long-Term Thinking

We build things meant to last — practices, organizations, and strategic frameworks that will serve our clients and patients for years, not quarters.
